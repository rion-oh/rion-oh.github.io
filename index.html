About

I am an undergraduate student in physics at KAIST, currently on exchange at the University of Washington. I am interested in understanding how galaxies form and evolve, particularly how star formation is regulated across cosmic time.

My research focuses on the physical mechanisms driving this regulation, including gas cycling, feedback, and galaxy-scale processes such as AGN activity and mergers.
