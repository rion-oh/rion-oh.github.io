Research Interests

I am interested in understanding how galaxies form and evolve, with a particular focus on how star formation is regulated and quenched across cosmic time. I am especially interested in the physical processes that govern galaxy quenching, including the role of gas depletion, feedback, and baryonic cycling in shaping galaxy evolution. More broadly, I am interested in early galaxy formation, star formation histories, AGN feedback, and dusty star-forming galaxies.
